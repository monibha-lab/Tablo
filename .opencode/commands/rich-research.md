Run this research pipeline in sequence:

**Phase 1 — Research** (@researcher)
Search the web for:
1. Top 15 occupations of people earning $500K+ annually (surgeons, hedge fund managers, PE partners, tech founders, real estate developers, corporate lawyers, investment bankers, CEOs, etc.)
2. For each occupation: what specific frustrations, problems, and time sinks do they repeatedly complain about — both professional (compliance burden, talent issues, deal flow) and personal (time, health, family, privacy, logistics)?
3. What premium services are they already spending heavily on? What are they still underserved in?
4. Search Reddit (r/fatFIRE, r/ChubbyFIRE, r/HENRYfinance), Bloomberg, Forbes, and niche forums for direct quotes and complaints.

Save findings to `research-output.md`.

**Phase 2 — Analysis** (@analyst)
Read `research-output.md` and:
1. Score each pain point on willingness to pay, urgency, and competition gap
2. Propose a specific premium service business for each high-scoring opportunity
3. Estimate pricing
4. Rank top 10 ideas

Save to `analysis-output.md`.

**Phase 3 — Final Report** (@synthesizer)
Read `research-output.md` and `analysis-output.md`.
Write a final polished business opportunity report.
Save to `final-report.md`.
